T.F. Green Airport (PVD) is located just outside of Providence, and is the closest airport to our hotel and venue. Just grab a Lyft or a taxi from the airport to downtown PVD!

Boston Logan Airport (BOS) offers a much wider variety of flights, and is approximately one hour away from Providence. If you rent a car, you can take I-95 South straight into Providence. If you prefer public transportation, you can take a free shuttle to Boston's South Station, and then take the MBTA Commuter Rail. From the Providence train station, our hotel is just a 5 minute walk away.
